Why did I create this?

It is easy to assume that the consumption of relevant new information will benefit your financial investments.

In reality, in an information driven world it is often more likely that reducing your information intake will lead you to a more refined, clear mental state that will allow you to make clear decisions without filtered biases or falling into the noise and whims of the crowd.
In truth, reducing reactionary, impulsive decisions will lead you to better outcomes over time. The practice of pulling away from information overload will give you an edge on the system, as well as improving your health and wellbeing.

May you make easy and simple gains, friend.
